Peace & Human Rights Advocacy

At ARAC International we champion a future where every person can live free from fear and want. Our mission is to promote sustainable development and the preservation of human rights through evidence‑based peace research and advocacy.

Our multidisciplinary teams conduct risk analyses in conflict zones, provide humanitarian aid support and develop policy recommendations aligned with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the U.S. Global Fragility Act. We work closely with partners around the world to foster a culture of peace, justice and human dignity.

Whether analysing political risk, advising on security management, or training communities on positive peace frameworks, ARAC International strives to empower individuals and organisations to build a more peaceful and sustainable world.
