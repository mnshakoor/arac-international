Our Mission

ARAC International champions a future where every person can live free from fear and want. Our mission is to promote sustainable development and the preservation of human rights through evidence-based peace research and advocacy.

Our teams conduct risk analyses in conflict zones, provide humanitarian aid support, and develop policy recommendations aligned with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the U.S. Global Fragility Act. We work globally to foster a culture of peace, justice, and human dignity.

Through partnerships and training, ARAC empowers communities and organizations to build resilience and strengthen governance for sustainable peace.
